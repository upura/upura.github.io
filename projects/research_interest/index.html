Pre-Trained Language Models and News Media

May 1, 2021

My research interest is to explore the opportunities and challenges of pre-trained language models in the news media.

Challenges in Practice

  • Monitoring time-series performance degradation ( AACL-IJCNLP 2022)
  • Training Data Extraction ( ACL 2023 Workshop)
  • Adversarial Validation ( SIGIR 2021 Workshop)

Applied Machine Learning

  • Editors-in-the-loop Summarization ( BigData 2021 Workshop)
  • Reading Time Estimation ( BigData 2022 Industrial & Government Track)
  • Multilingual News Similarity Scoring ( NAACL 2022 Workshop)
  • Next Item Recommendation ( WSDM 2021 Workshop)
  • Quantifying Diachronic Language Change ( Ishihara et al., IC2S2 2023)
